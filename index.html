Ansys HFSS

Evaluation of SAR in human head from an RF Transmitter coil
  • An RF transmitter coil with a rectangular helix is placed near a human head model to evaluate SAR (Specific Absorption Rate) and E & H field interactions in biological tissue.
  • The simulation operates at 300 kHz, utilizing an iterative solver with a convergence criteria of 0.1% max Delta energy. A parametric sweep is performed to assess the effect of coil orientation on SAR and E & H field distribution
  • SAR heatmaps are generated, identifying hot spots and potential safety concerns. These results are used to optimize the coil design, ensuring SAR compliance for medical RF devices and enhancing both safety and performance
